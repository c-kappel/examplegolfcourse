Course Information

Experience our championship 18-hole course

Course Overview

Landscape Links is a premier 18-hole championship golf course nestled in the heart of Richmond, Virginia. Our meticulously maintained course offers a challenging yet enjoyable experience for golfers of all skill levels.

Course Details

  • Par: 72
  • Length: 6,847 yards from the championship tees
  • Course Rating: 73.2
  • Slope Rating: 135
  • Architect: Robert Trent Jones II
  • Year Opened: 1998

Course Features

Our course features rolling fairways, strategic bunkering, and pristine bentgrass greens that provide a true and consistent roll. Water comes into play on seven holes, adding both beauty and challenge to your round.

The front nine winds through mature hardwood forests, offering a peaceful, tree-lined experience. The back nine opens up with panoramic views of the surrounding countryside, featuring more dramatic elevation changes and strategic design elements.

Practice Facilities

  • Full-service driving range with covered and grass tee stations
  • Large practice putting green
  • Short game area with bunker practice
  • Chipping green

Course Maintenance

Our dedicated grounds crew ensures the course is maintained to the highest standards year-round. We utilize cutting-edge agronomy practices and sustainable maintenance techniques to provide optimal playing conditions while respecting the environment.
