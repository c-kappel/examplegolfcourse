Golf Policy

Guidelines for an enjoyable experience for all

Tee Time Reservations

  • Tee times may be reserved up to 7 days in advance for members, 3 days for non-members
  • Reservations can be made by phone at (555) 867-5309 or online through our booking system
  • Please arrive at least 15 minutes prior to your tee time
  • Groups larger than 4 players require advance approval from the golf shop

Cancellation Policy

  • Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours in advance
  • Late cancellations or no-shows may result in loss of booking privileges
  • In case of inclement weather, the golf shop will contact you regarding course conditions

Dress Code

We maintain a traditional dress code to preserve the integrity and atmosphere of the club:

Acceptable Attire

  • Collared shirts or mock turtlenecks (tucked in preferred)
  • Golf slacks, khakis, or tailored shorts (no more than 4 inches above the knee)
  • Golf skirts or skorts for ladies
  • Soft-spiked or spikeless golf shoes

Not Permitted

  • Denim or cargo shorts
  • T-shirts or tank tops
  • Athletic wear (unless specifically designed for golf)
  • Metal spikes

Pace of Play

We strive to maintain a pace of play that allows all golfers to enjoy their round. Please observe the following guidelines:

  • 18 holes should be completed in approximately 4 hours and 15 minutes
  • Be ready to play when it's your turn
  • Keep up with the group ahead of you
  • If you fall behind, allow faster groups to play through
  • Limit practice swings and time spent searching for lost balls (3 minutes maximum)

Course Etiquette

  • Repair ball marks on greens and divots on fairways
  • Rake bunkers after use
  • Keep golf carts on designated paths when posted
  • Avoid slow play - be considerate of groups behind you
  • Silence cell phones on the course
  • Respect other players by avoiding unnecessary noise and movement

Cart Policy

  • Carts must remain on paths when "cart path only" signs are posted
  • Observe 90-degree rule when applicable
  • Keep carts away from greens, tees, and hazards
  • No more than 2 riders per cart
  • Drivers must be at least 16 years old with a valid driver's license

Practice Areas

  • Driving range opens at 7:00 AM
  • Range balls must be purchased from the golf shop
  • Use only range balls on the driving range
  • Practice putting green is available for all golfers
  • Chipping area is for short game practice only - full swings not permitted

Weather Policy

  • In case of lightning, immediately seek shelter
  • Air horn signals: 1 blast = suspend play, 3 blasts = resume play
  • Rain checks will be issued for rounds interrupted by weather
  • Course closures due to weather will be posted on our website and social media

Children Policy

  • Junior golfers under 13 must be accompanied by an adult
  • Children must demonstrate adequate golf skills to play on the course
  • Junior rates apply to golfers age 17 and under
  • Non-playing children are not permitted on the golf course

Note: Management reserves the right to modify these policies at any time. Thank you for helping us maintain Landscape Links as a premier golf facility!
