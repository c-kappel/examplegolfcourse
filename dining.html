Dining at Landscape Links

Exceptional cuisine in an elegant setting

The Clubhouse Restaurant

Our clubhouse restaurant offers a refined dining experience with panoramic views of the 18th hole. Whether you're enjoying a post-round meal with friends or celebrating a special occasion, our culinary team delivers exceptional cuisine crafted from locally-sourced ingredients.

Hours of Operation

  • Breakfast: 7:00 AM – 11:00 AM (Weekends only)
  • Lunch: 11:00 AM – 4:00 PM (Daily)
  • Dinner: 5:00 PM – 9:00 PM (Thursday – Sunday)
  • Bar: 11:00 AM – 10:00 PM (Daily)

Located at the turn between the 9th and 10th holes, The Turn Tavern offers quick service for golfers looking to grab a bite mid-round.

  • Hours: 8:00 AM – 6:00 PM
