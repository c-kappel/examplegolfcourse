Dining at Landscape Links

Exceptional cuisine in an elegant setting

The Clubhouse Restaurant

Our clubhouse restaurant offers a refined dining experience with panoramic views of the 18th hole. Whether you're enjoying a post-round meal with friends or celebrating a special occasion, our culinary team delivers exceptional cuisine crafted from locally-sourced ingredients.

Hours of Operation

  • Breakfast: 7:00 AM - 11:00 AM (Weekends only)
  • Lunch: 11:00 AM - 4:00 PM (Daily)
  • Dinner: 5:00 PM - 9:00 PM (Thursday - Sunday)
  • Bar: 11:00 AM - 10:00 PM (Daily)

The Turn Tavern

Located at the turn between the 9th and 10th holes, The Turn Tavern offers quick service for golfers looking to grab a bite mid-round. Enjoy hot dogs, sandwiches, snacks, and refreshing beverages.

  • Hours: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM (Daily during golf season)
  • Hot Dog: $5
  • Sandwich: $8
  • Snacks & Beverages: $2-4

Private Events & Catering

Our elegant banquet facilities can accommodate groups from 20 to 200 guests for weddings, corporate events, tournaments, and celebrations. Our experienced event coordinators will work with you to create a memorable experience.

Event Services Include:

  • Customized menus for any occasion
  • Full-service bar options
  • Professional event coordination
  • Audio/visual equipment
  • Indoor and outdoor venue options

For event inquiries, contact our Events Coordinator at (555) 867-5309 ext. 3 or email events@landscapelinks.com

Reservations

Reservations are recommended for dinner service, especially on weekends. Walk-ins are welcome based on availability. Call (555) 867-5309 to reserve your table today!
