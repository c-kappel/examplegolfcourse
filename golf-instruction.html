Golf Instruction

Improve your game with our PGA professionals

Our Teaching Professionals

At Landscape Links, we're proud to offer instruction from PGA-certified professionals who are dedicated to helping golfers of all skill levels improve their game. Whether you're picking up a club for the first time or looking to refine your competitive edge, our instructors provide personalized guidance tailored to your goals.

Technology & Facilities

Our instruction facility features state-of-the-art technology to help you understand and improve your swing:

  • TrackMan launch monitor for detailed shot analysis
  • High-speed video analysis with V1 software
  • Indoor hitting bay for year-round instruction
  • Putting lab with SAM PuttLab technology

Junior Programs

We're committed to growing the game by offering comprehensive junior programs:

  • Junior Academy (Ages 8-17): Year-round development program
  • Golf & Go (Ages 5-7): Introduction to golf fundamentals
  • Summer Golf Camp: Week-long intensive programs
  • High School Team Support: Practice facilities and coaching available
